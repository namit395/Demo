Mahonty Acedamy of Commerce offers fully residential co-education with the blend of deep –rooted ethos of our ancient Indian culture and the most contemporary teaching methodologies designed by International Baccalaureate. Mahonty Acedamy of Commerce stresses an overall development of the students by carefully balancing the student’s academic, activities, sports, performing and aesthetics arts in the state of art infrastructure and facilities available within the campus.
Admission to Mahonty Acedamy of Commerce is strictly based on the students to cope up with the educational programs offered at the school.
STD. XI & Gr. XII XI & XII (commerce) Only
Residential
Admission will be strictly based on the personal interview with student & Parent.
Applicants applying for the admissions to XI & XII, will be appearing for an aptitude test in basic English and Mathematics in addition to the personal interview.
Send the duly filled-in form with the following enclosures:
      • Download the Registration form and an admission form
      • Photograph pasted on the registration form.
      • Copy of the birth certificate
      • Copy of the last academic reports on receipt of an admission form, we will invite you for the campus visit and to attend an aptitude test / interview once selected, pay the registration charges and an admission fee to confirm an admission.
      • Composite annual fee and other components of the fee to be paid a month before the start of the school
